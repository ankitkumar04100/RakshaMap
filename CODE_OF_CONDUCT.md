We are committed to creating a **safe, inclusive, and respectful environment** for all contributors, users, and stakeholders of RakshaMap. By participating, you agree to follow this code of conduct.

---

## Our Standards

1. **Respect & Empathy**
   - Treat everyone with kindness, professionalism, and understanding.
   - Value differing opinions and experiences.

2. **Constructive Feedback**
   - Offer feedback that is helpful, clear, and courteous.
   - Avoid personal attacks, derogatory comments, or judgment.

3. **Inclusivity**
   - Ensure accessibility and usability for all genders, backgrounds, and abilities.
   - Avoid discriminatory or exclusionary behavior.

4. **Ethical Practices**
   - Adhere to ethical AI and privacy-first principles.
   - Avoid actions that may compromise user safety or community trust.

5. **Reporting**
   - Report violations, harassment, or unethical behavior to repository maintainers.
   - Support a safe and positive environment for everyone.

---

## Enforcement

Violations of this code may result in:
- Warnings or guidance
- Removal of contributions
- Temporary or permanent exclusion from project collaboration

---

## Our Commitment

RakshaMap fosters a community that is **inclusive, ethical, and impact-driven**, ensuring contributors and users can focus on building **safe and socially responsible AI solutions**.
