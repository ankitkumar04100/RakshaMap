Thank you for your interest in contributing to **RakshaMap**, the AI-powered safety intelligence platform. We welcome all contributors who share our vision of building ethical, inclusive, and socially impactful technology.

---

## How You Can Contribute

1. **Code & Development**
   - Enhance AI prediction models and algorithms.
   - Optimize backend APIs for real-time performance.
   - Improve frontend UI/UX for better accessibility and clarity.

2. **Data & Research**
   - Validate simulated datasets.
   - Contribute insights or open-source datasets.
   - Assist in ethical data preprocessing and feature engineering.

3. **Documentation**
   - Improve README, guides, or tutorials.
   - Add diagrams and examples for AI workflow or system architecture.

4. **Testing & Quality Assurance**
   - Report bugs or unexpected behaviors.
   - Suggest improvements for AI predictions and map visualizations.
   - Validate safety alerts, route recommendations, and real-time updates.

5. **Community & Outreach**
   - Advocate for ethical AI practices.
   - Collect feedback from users and NGOs.
   - Help in localizing the platform for new cities or regions.

---

## Our Expectations

- Maintain **respectful, inclusive communication**.
- Follow **ethical AI practices** in all contributions.
- Ensure contributions do not compromise **user privacy or safety**.
- Submit **well-documented, tested code** when possible.

---

## Getting Started

1. Fork the repository.
2. Clone it locally.
3. Create a feature branch (`git checkout -b feature/your-feature`).
4. Commit changes (`git commit -m "Add feature"`).
5. Push to the branch (`git push origin feature/your-feature`).
6. Open a Pull Request describing your contribution.

---

RakshaMap thrives when contributions are collaborative, thoughtful, and socially responsible. Thank you for helping us **predict, prevent, and protect** communities.
